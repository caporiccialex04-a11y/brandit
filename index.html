Our story

An international award-winning branding agency since 2010.

BRANDiT is a brand consultancy design studio specialising in brand strategy, brand identity, and multichannel brand experience design. From standout identities to sharper digital experiences, we support organisations through every stage of brand transformation.

Our pedigree runs deep. The founder cut his design teeth at BRANDCENTRAL, Beirut’s leading branding unit at the time, after earning a merit from the International Society for Typographic Designers in London.

Less than a year after opening, BRANDiT received its first international award — then a second, third, and fourth — from competitions as far afield as Italy and the United States. Today we work from Beirut and Dubai with clients across the GCC and beyond.
